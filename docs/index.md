## Purpose

To accurately evaluate your environment and provide you with an accurate proposal and preliminary framework for the complete scope of the project, the F5 sales team requests information related to your infrastructure and applications currently being considered for protection.

!!! info "How to Complete This Guide"
    Please complete each section to the best of your knowledge. If you have any questions, please contact your F5 Specialist or Sales Engineer.
